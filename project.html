Dhriti – From Plastic to Purpose

Duration: Mar 2025 - Present

Description: Dhriti is a purpose-driven initiative I’m proud to be working on — a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between local plastic waste collectors and industrial buyers in India. Our country generates over 3 million tons of plastic waste annually, yet industries often import plastic from abroad because it is well-categorized and easier to process. Meanwhile, local vendors and kabadiwalas collect tons of plastic from streets and homes, but lack a direct connection to industries that need it.

Platform Goals:

  • Empower local collectors and citizens to earn by selling plastic waste.
  • Help industries reduce plastic import dependence by sourcing categorized plastic locally.
  • Promote plastic segregation at source, reducing pollution and encouraging responsible disposal.
  • Digitize the informal waste sector, bringing them into the formal economy.

Key Features:

  • User-friendly plastic category uploads
  • Vendor-industrial buyer matching
  • Pickup scheduling and fair pricing models
  • AI-based sorting suggestions and gamified rewards

Skills Gained: Research Writing · Cross-Cultural Communication · Environmental Awareness · Presentations

Impact: Supports Swachh Bharat, Atmanirbhar Bharat, and Circular Economy initiatives; turns waste into worth, and pollution into purpose.
